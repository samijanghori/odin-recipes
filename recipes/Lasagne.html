Lasagne

Description

Lasagne is a classic Italian baked pasta dish with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese.

This recipe will guide you in making a delicious and hearty lasagne perfect for family meals.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagne noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups meat sauce (beef or tomato-based)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cook lasagne noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish.
  4. Add a layer of noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, and repeat until all ingredients are used.
  5. Top with Parmesan cheese and bake for 30-40 minutes until golden and bubbly.
