Rice

Description

Rice is a versatile staple food enjoyed around the world. It can be served as a side dish, a main dish, or even in desserts.

This recipe will guide you through cooking perfect fluffy rice every time.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil (optional)

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. Combine rice, water, and salt in a pot.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and cover.
  4. Simmer for 15-20 minutes until water is absorbed.
  5. Remove from heat and let it rest for 5 minutes before serving.
