pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made of a yeasted flatbread topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings.

This recipe will show you how to make a simple homemade pizza from scratch.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1-2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite toppings (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll out the dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle cheese and add toppings.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is melted.
